One Direction

The biggest boy band in the world.

Photo courtesy of Wikipedia.

Formed in 2012 on singing television competition The X Factor, One Direction is an English-Irish boy band known for catchy pop songs, good looks, and charming personalities. The group made waves with their debut album Up All Night, putting them on the global music scene. Five albums, four world tours, and numerous awards and chart-topping singles later, One Direction is known to many fans as the biggest boy band in history.
